Privacy Policy for PlantSaga

Last Updated: October 2025

At PlantSaga, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as your name, email address (team.plantsaga@gmail.com), and photos of plants you upload for identification.

2. Use of AI Technology

PlantSaga uses AI to analyze images for plant identification and health diagnosis. These images are processed securely.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is only shared with essential service providers (e.g., cloud storage) to keep the app running.

4. Account Deletion

You may request to delete your account and all associated plant data at any time via the app settings or by emailing us.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions, please contact us at: team.plantsaga@gmail.com
