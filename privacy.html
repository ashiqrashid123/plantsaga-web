Privacy Policy

Effective: February 17, 2026

At PlantSaga, we believe every plant has a story, and so does your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy while helping your garden grow.

1. Data Collection

We only collect what's necessary for botanical care:

  • Account Info: Name and email provided by you.
  • Growth Logs: Photos and health data of your plants.
  • Analytics: How you use our AI features to better the community.

2. How We Use Data

Your data powers the intelligence of the community:

  • To provide tailored care reminders.
  • To improve AI plant identification accuracy.
  • To secure your digital garden across devices.

3. Your Rights

You are in complete control of your data:

  • Access: Request all data we hold.
  • Erasure: Delete your account and all data instantly.
  • Portability: Download your garden history anytime.
